About Mink Coin

I created Mink Coin to transform lives, starting with my own, while giving back to the community. My passion for crypto and its vibrant community inspired me to develop something that I can share and enjoy with others. Before the lockdown, I was unaware of crypto, but once I understood it, I was eager to dive deeper. Having experienced setbacks with other projects, I was determined to create something reliable and engagingâ€”Mink Coin.

Mink Coin is not just another "meme" coin or speculative ERC-20 token; it is a "ME" coin, representing the energy and vision of its creator, Minkola. My goal is to grow this project, build a genuine community, and demonstrate that Mink Coin is not a fleeting venture. I aim to bring people together, improve mental health through community interaction, gaming, teamwork, and a sense of belongining.

This project began as a mere idea to create a coin, but I want to show the public that it can be so much more. With over 20 years of experience in IT, I have numerous ideas that I am eager to bring to life. I don't want to be a couch potato; I want Mink Coin to have real utility.

I am starting with Mink Coin and will soon introduce seasonal NFTs featuring creative and funky representations of myself. These NFTs will be available for purchase each month, offering continuous engagement. Following the NFT launch, I plan to open an online store where people can buy merchandise like T-shirts, hoodies, baseball caps, tea coasters, candles, cookies, and brownies using Mink Coin. I have been networking with suppliers to facilitate this, and any profits generated will be reinvested into the DAO/pool to further grow the project. Additionally, I will be regularly updating the website to enhance the user experience.

Mink Coin is a community-driven project designed to create a positive and enjoyable environment for all its participants. Join us, and be part of something more than just a cryptocurrency.
