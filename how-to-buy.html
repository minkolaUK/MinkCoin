How to Buy Mink Coin

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Set Up a Crypto Wallet

If you don't already have a crypto wallet, you'll need to set one up. We recommend using MetaMask:

Go to the MetaMask Website and download the wallet extension for your browser. Follow the instructions to create a new wallet and secure your recovery phrase. Please make sure you select ETC (Ethereum Classic) when transferring your ETC from your exchange account to your MetaMask wallet address. Never share your recovery phrase with anyone!

Step 2: Add Ethereum Classic Network

Add the Ethereum Classic network to MetaMask by clicking the network dropdown at the top left corner and selecting + Add network. Enter the following details:

  • Network Name: Ethereum Classic
  • New RPC URL: https://www.ethercluster.com/etc
  • Chain ID: 61
  • Symbol: ETC
  • Block Explorer URL: https://blockscout.com/etc/mainnet

Click Save.

Step 3: Buy Ethereum Classic (ETC)

Purchase Ethereum Classic (ETC) from a cryptocurrency exchange such as Binance, Kraken, Bitget, or Coinbase. Once you have ETC, send it to your MetaMask wallet address.

Step 4: Swap ETC for Mink Coin

Use HebeSwap to swap ETC for Mink Coin. Go to Hebe Swap, connect your MetaMask wallet, and enter the amount of ETC you want to swap. Complete the transaction and import Mink Coin into MetaMask using the token address: 0x89E50A895d0FE5e5A278B2935225f50c063Ef372.
